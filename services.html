Our Services

Integrated engineering, manufacturing, and sourcing solutions.

PCB Manufacturing

EtonTech is your One-Stop Shop for Quick-Turn PCB manufacturing and PCB Assembly Services. We provide companies large and small with high quality Printed Circuit Boards and Full Turnkey PCB Assemblies. Our state-of-the-art SMT lines minimize handwork.

Quick-Turn CNC Machining

CNC MACHINES make prototypes efficiently, significantly reducing time to market. We offer various CNC machining services including milling, turning, EDM and wire EDM, surface grinding and much more for both metal and nonmetal.

Injection Molding

Injection molding provides low cost at moderate to large quantities, making it an extremely versatile process for a wide range of parts with an excellent finish. Our team of expert engineers and mold makers turns your CAD models into reality.

Sourcing

EtonTech focuses on obtaining a low "first cost" from the factory source as part of a transparent and highly efficient supply chain, offering value based on the experience and relationships of its professional staff and partners.

Engineering Service

Experience engineering design assistance and complete PCB layout design services. Getting involved early in product development and working closely with engineers, EtonTech works aggressively to exceed goals and time-to-market constraints.

Embedded Development

EtonTech offers a range of services for embedded system development. Based on customer needs, we develop parts or the entirety of embedded systems, integrating firmware and manufacturing capabilities seamlessly.
